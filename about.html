Ms. Rowena Teresa Antenorcruz

President

She is a psychologist who holds a Masters in Applied Psychology and a Doctorate degree of Clinical Psychology in the Ateneo De Manila University and the University of the Philippines, respectively. She has plentiful experience working with individuals with a multitude of psychological problems. Rowena is well-trained in the field wherein she has encountered patients who were victims of rape, pscyhological, mental and physical abuse, and women who needed help of any kind. Because of her exposure to these victims, she has decided to come up with an organization that focuses on women empowerment together with Ms. Raya Sabandal as the General Affairs Vice President. She manages the organization and spearheads all the campaigns, activities and events that the association intends to commit. She is inspired by the quote stated by Maya Angelou, "Each time a woman stands up for herself, She stands up for all women."



Ms. Raya Marie Sabandal

General Affairs Vice President

She graduated Bachelor of Arts with the degree of Bachelor of Laws from De La Salle University. She serves the country for 4 years already. Raya already stood in different case for every woman just like rape cases, human trafficking and prostitution. She is the general affairs vice president for EmpowerHer. In addition, she is also the co-founder of the said organization. Being a part of the organization gives her the freedom to fight for the rights of every woman.
